Summary of Teaching Philosophy

I believe physics is a powerful framework for cultivating disciplined reasoning, analytical thinking, and intellectual curiosity. My teaching emphasizes inclusivity, rigor, and student-centered engagement. I aim to create learning environments where students from diverse backgrounds feel both challenged and supported in building the skills necessary for scientific and academic success.
