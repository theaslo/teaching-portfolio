Student Evaluations

I regularly collect student feedback through official SETs and mid-semester reflections. These evaluations highlight my commitment to clarity, supportiveness, and rigorous instruction across diverse physics courses at UConn.

Representative Student Comments

“The instructor was very kind and interested in the students’ success.”
“This professor makes it feel easier. I like how she teaches.”
“She always made sure all questions were answered and heard as well as student feedback.”
“She is extremely dedicated and gave me the confidence to work hard and succeed.”

“Physics has never made more sense to me than it does now.”

Quantitative Ratings

Across multiple semesters and course levels, students consistently rate the following above 4.5/5:

  • “The instructor communicated ideas and information clearly.”
  • “The instructor was approachable and responsive to student questions.”
  • “The instructor created a learning environment that supported my success.”
  • “I learned a lot in this course.”
