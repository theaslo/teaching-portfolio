Student Evaluations

Student feedback highlights my approachability, dedication, and effectiveness in supporting learning. Below are selected student quotes from recent SETs (Student Evaluation of Teaching):

“The instructor was very kind and interested in the students’ success.”
“This professor makes it feel easier. I like how she teaches.”
“She always made sure all questions were answered and heard as well as student feedback.”
“She is extremely dedicated and gave me the confidence to work hard and succeed.”

I use mid-semester feedback to adjust pacing, integrate new supports, and revise instructional methods in real-time. Evaluation scores remain consistently strong across both introductory and upper-division physics courses.
