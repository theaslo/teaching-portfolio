Courses Taught

University of Connecticut – Hartford Campus

  • PHYS 1020Q: Introductory Astronomy – General education course exploring astrophysical concepts for non-STEM majors.

  • PHYS 1201Q & PHYS 1202Q: General Physics I & II with Labs – Algebra-based physics for life science and pre-med students. Emphasis on conceptual understanding, foundational skills, and lab inquiry.

University of Connecticut – Storrs Campus

  • PHYS 3201: Electricity and Magnetism I – A mathematically rigorous course for physics majors covering vector calculus, electrostatics, and Maxwell's equations.
