Courses Taught

University of Connecticut – Hartford Campus

  • PHYS 1020Q: Introductory Astronomy – General education course exploring astrophysical concepts for non-STEM majors.
  • PHYS 1030Q: Physics of the Environment – Focused on climate systems, energy, and environmental physics.
  • PHYS 1201Q & PHYS 1202Q: General Physics I & II with Labs – Algebra-based physics sequence for life science and pre-med students. Emphasis on conceptual understanding, foundational skills, and experimental modeling.

University of Connecticut – Storrs Campus

  • PHYS 3201: Electricity and Magnetism I – A mathematically rigorous course for physics majors covering vector calculus, electrostatics, and Maxwell's equations.

Innovations in Course Delivery

  • Active learning sessions with live polling and whiteboard group work
  • Integrated math skills review (algebra, trigonometry) in scaffolded instruction
  • Model Context Protocol (MCP) tools supporting physics computation, coding, and GenAI learning engagement
